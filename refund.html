Refund policy

Kushiara Travels Ltd values customer satisfaction and understands that sometimes plans can change. Our refund policy has been designed to be fair and transparent to all of our customers.

Refundable Services:

  • Flight tickets
  • Accommodation
  • Transportation

Non-Refundable Services:

  • Visas
  • Travel insurance

Refund Process:

  • To initiate a refund, customers must notify Kushiara Travels Ltd via email or phone within the specified timeframe for each service.
  • Refund requests will be processed within 7-14 business days from the date of receipt.
  • Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used to purchase the services.
  • In case of a refundable service being partially used, a partial refund will be issued based on the terms and conditions of the service provider.

Cancellation policy:

  • Cancellation requests must be received in writing via email or phone.
  • The amount of the refund will be based on the cancellation policy of the service provider and the date of cancellation.
  • For cancellations made within 24 hours of purchase, a full refund will be issued.
  • For cancellations made after 24 hours of purchase, a processing fee of X% may apply.
  • No show and early departure fees are non-refundable.

Note:

  • The refund policy is subject to change without prior notice.
  • Any charges imposed by the service providers (airlines, hotels, etc.) for cancellations or changes are the responsibility of the customer.
  • Refund amounts may be subject to a processing fee.

Kushiara Travels Ltd is committed to providing the best possible customer experience and will make every effort to resolve any issues in a timely and fair manner. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
